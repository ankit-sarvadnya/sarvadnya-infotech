Today's session focused on integrating the "Quick Access Hub" into the landing page, refining the navigation experience, and providing experimental tools for the demo environment. We also addressed several UI/UX issues related to spacing and mobile responsiveness.

- **Mobile Responsiveness Fix:** Addressed a typing animation bug on mobile devices where the animated cursor would wrap to a new line. Applied `whitespace-nowrap` to ensure the text and cursor stay on the same line during the animation.
