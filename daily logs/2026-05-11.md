## Technical Context for Future Sessions (AI Prompt)

- **Hero Logic:** The `HomeHero` uses a complex state-machine for transitions. If adding new slides, ensure both `titleText` (for typing) and `gradient` (for styling) are provided in `heroContents`.
- **Navigation Sync:** Always update `lib/product-nav.ts` when adding new services; the `Productbar` dynamically renders based on this file.
- **Mobile First:** The `Productbar` is extremely tight on space. When adding new categories, keep the label under 10 characters or use the `hidden min-[480px]:block` pattern for icons.
- **Typing Sync:** The typing effect in `HomeHero` has a `1300ms` delay to wait for the staggered entry. Adjust this if changing the `delay-[250ms]` or transition duration.
