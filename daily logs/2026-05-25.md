# Daily Log - May 25, 2026

## Overview
Today marked a significant architectural and visual milestone for Sarvadnya Infotech. We executed a complete brand identity transition, moving from the legacy dark indigo theme to a modern, high-energy **Blue, Sky & White** palette. In parallel, the search engine was transformed into an intelligent, proactive assistant with deep-linking capabilities and AI-generated summaries.

## Core Accomplishments

### 1. Intelligent Search Transformation (v1.1.135 - v1.1.136)
- **AI-Generated Overviews:** Integrated a "Quick AI Answer" feature that provides a minimalist, Google-style summary at the top of search results, explaining how Sarvadnya's services match the user's query.
- **Deep-Linking Engine:** Upgraded the search backend to provide exact, direct links. Modules now link to specific details (`/modules?id=...`) and services to their dedicated sub-pages (AMC, TDL, etc.), eliminating generic redirects.
- **"No Match" Intelligence:** Implemented specific UI feedback for cases where no title match is found, distinguishing between partial matches and exact results.
- **AI Sitemap:** Created a comprehensive `SITEMAP.md` in the root directory to serve as a foundational knowledge map for the AI Assistant and improved user discoverability.

### 2. Major Brand Re-Identity (v1.1.137 - v1.1.139)
- **Palette Transition:** Successfully migrated the entire platform from the legacy dark theme to a professional light-based system.
    - **Old Palette:** Indigo (`#7f00ff`), Dark Purple (`#0a041a`).
    - **New Palette (v1.1.139 Milestone):**
        - **Light Blue (#E9F1FA):** Used for navigation backgrounds and primary UI foundations.
        - **Bright Blue (#00ABE4):** Used for high-action buttons, badges, and interactive highlights.
        - **Deep Blue (#0371a3):** Used for extreme contrast text, authority gradients, and primary CTAs.
        - **Sky Tint (#f0f9ff):** Used for atmospheric depth, hover states, and secondary card backgrounds.
        - **White (#FFFFFF):** Used as the pure base and for high-legibility contrast.
- **Navbar & Productbar Polish:**
    - Integrated a small, square, rounded logo (derived from high-res `logo.png`) continuously with the brand name.
    - **Productbar Scale:** Increased desktop height by 30% (`36px`) for better visibility while maintaining compact `20px` sizing for mobile.
    - **Mobile Logic:** Hides the square logo on mobile while keeping the "Certified" badge and name visible for a cleaner vertical layout.

### 3. Homepage-wide Synchronization (v1.1.139)
- **Softened Background:** Reduced the Hero `ShapeGrid` opacity to **0.1** to create a subtle, premium texture.
- **Component Refresh:** Systematically updated `HomeHero`, `HomeStat`, `QuickAccessHub`, `FAQ`, and `SupportButton` to utilize the new expanded Blue palette.
- **Design Checkpoint:** Established **v1.1.139** as the formal restoration point for this refined visual identity.
